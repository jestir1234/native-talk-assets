Capítulo 1 – La Siesta Misteriosa
Isabella, a curious girl in Granada, falls asleep after lunch with her abuela.
In her nap, she hears soft voices calling her to the plaza at midnight.
Language focus: daily routines (almorzar, dormir la siesta), time (son las doce).

Capítulo 2 – La Puerta en la Plaza
At midnight, Isabella sneaks out and discovers an arch glowing behind the market stalls.
When she whispers “sueño”, the arch opens like a door.
Language focus: greetings, places in town (plaza, mercado, calle).

Capítulo 3 – Los Vendedores Extraños
Inside, the market is alive: a woman sells sueños de alegría, a man sells recuerdos de viajes.
Fruits glow, books whisper secrets, lanterns float in the air.
Language focus: shopping words (¿Cuánto cuesta? Quiero…), common nouns.

Capítulo 4 – El Niño sin Sueños
Isabella meets a boy who sadly explains that he has lost all his dreams.
He asks Isabella to help him find a way to dream again.
Language focus: emotions (estoy triste, tengo miedo, estoy feliz).

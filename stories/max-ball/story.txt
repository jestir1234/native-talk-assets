Max is a dog.
He has a red ball.

Max likes his ball.
He plays every day.

He runs and jumps.
The ball goes bounce, bounce!

The ball rolls under the chair.
“Oh no!” says Max.

Max looks under the chair.
He can’t reach it.

Mom comes to help.
She gets the ball.

“Thank you, Mom!” says Max.
He is happy again.

Max plays with his ball.
The sun is bright.

He gets sleepy.
He hugs the ball and sleeps.

Good night, Max.
Good night, red ball.

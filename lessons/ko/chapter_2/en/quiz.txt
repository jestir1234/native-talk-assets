# Chapter 2 Quiz: Basic Greetings and Introductions

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: What does "안녕하세요" mean?
options:
  - Goodbye
  - Hello
  - Thank you
  - Please
correctAnswer: Hello
explanation: "안녕하세요" is the formal way to say "Hello" in Korean.

## Question 2
type: fillInTheBlank
question: How do you say "My name is" in Korean?
options:
  - 저는 ___입니다
  - 안녕하세요
  - 감사합니다
  - 네
correctAnswer: 저는 ___입니다
explanation: "저는 ___입니다" means "My name is ___" in Korean.

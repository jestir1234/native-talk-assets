# Japanese Chapter 2 Quiz: Hiragana – The Vowel Sounds

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: Which hiragana character represents the sound "a"?
options:
  - あ
  - い
  - う
  - え
correctAnswer: あ
explanation: あ (a) is the first vowel in the Japanese hiragana syllabary.

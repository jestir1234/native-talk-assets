# Japanese Chapter 10 Quiz: Your First Japanese Paragraph

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: What does わたしはまいにち６じにおきます mean?
options:
  - I wake up at 6 every day
  - I go to bed at 6 every day
  - I eat at 6 every day
  - I study at 6 every day
correctAnswer: I wake up at 6 every day
explanation: わたしはまいにち６じにおきます (watashi wa mainichi roku-ji ni okimasu) means "I wake up at 6 every day."

## Question 5
type: multipleChoice
question: What particle is used to connect two actions in Japanese?
options:
  - て
  - に

  - は
correctAnswer: て
explanation: The て form is used to connect two actions in sequence.

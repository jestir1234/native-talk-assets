# Japanese Chapter 9 Quiz: Daily Life Vocabulary & Polite Requests

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: What does みずをください mean?
options:
  - Please give me water
  - I want water
  - I have water
  - I drink water
correctAnswer: Please give me water
explanation: みずをください (mizu wo kudasai) means "Please give me water" using the polite request form ください.

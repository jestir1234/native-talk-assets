# 10장 퀴즈: 시간과 일정

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: "몇 시인가요?"를 영어로 어떻게 말하나요?
options:
  - What time is it?
  - How time is it?
  - What hour is it?
  - How many time is it?
correctAnswer: What time is it?
explanation: "What time is it?"는 "몇 시인가요?"를 묻는 방법입니다.

# English Chapter 4 Quiz: 颜色和描述

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: 英语中询问物体颜色的标准问法是什么？
options:
  - What is the color?
  - What color is it?
  - Which color is it?
  - How color is it?
correctAnswer: What color is it?
explanation: "What color is it?"是询问物体颜色的标准表达。

correctAnswer: 红色

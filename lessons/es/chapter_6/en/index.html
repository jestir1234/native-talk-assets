Cultural Tip: Mealtimes! ü•ò

In many Spanish-speaking countries, especially Spain, dinner (cena) is eaten much later than in English-speaking countries, often around 9 PM or 10 PM. Lunch (almuerzo/comida) is usually the largest meal of the day!

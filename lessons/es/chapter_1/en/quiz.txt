# Spanish Chapter 1 Quiz: El Alfabeto Español y los Sonidos

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: How many letters does the Spanish alphabet have?
options:
  - 26
  - 27
  - 28
  - 29
correctAnswer: 27
explanation: The Spanish alphabet has 27 letters, including the ñ (eñe).

## Question 2
type: multipleChoice
question: What are the vowels in Spanish?
options:
  - A, E, I, O, U
  - A, E, I, O, U, Y
  - A, E, I, O, U, Ñ
  - A, E, I, O, U, Á, É, Í, Ó, Ú
correctAnswer: A, E, I, O, U
explanation: The vowels in Spanish are A, E, I, O, U. Y is sometimes considered a vowel, but technically there are 5 vowels.

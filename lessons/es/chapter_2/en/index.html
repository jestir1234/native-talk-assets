Introducing Yourself üëã

Time to tell people who you are! Here are two common ways:

Me llamo... [meh YAH-moh] My name is...

(Literally: "I call myself...")

Soy... [SOY] I am...

(Use "Soy" for professions, nationalities, or a quicker introduction)

Example:

  • Me llamo Ana. (My name is Ana.)
  • Soy David. (I am David.)

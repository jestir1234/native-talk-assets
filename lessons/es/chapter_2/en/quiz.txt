# Spanish Chapter 2 Quiz: Pronunciación y Palabras Básicas

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: How is the letter "H" pronounced in Spanish?
options:
  - Like in English
  - It's silent
  - Like "ch"
  - Like "h" in "hot"
correctAnswer: It's silent
explanation: The letter "H" is silent in Spanish. For example, "hola" is pronounced "ola".

# Chapter 2 Quiz: Basic Greetings and Introductions

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: What does "你好" mean?
options:
  - Goodbye
  - Hello
  - Thank you
  - Please
correctAnswer: Hello
explanation: "你好" (nǐ hǎo) is the standard way to say "Hello" in Chinese.

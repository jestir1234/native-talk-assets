Cultural Note: Weather's Impact in China üá®üá≥

China is vast, so weather varies significantly by region! For example, northern cities like Beijing experience very cold, dry winters with snow, and hot, humid summers. Southern cities like Guangzhou have mild winters and extremely hot, humid summers with heavy rainfall.

Weather forecasts are very popular on news and apps, as they often dictate daily plans, especially concerning travel and outdoor activities. People often discuss the weather as a common polite greeting or small talk.

# Chapter 9 Quiz: Weather and Seasons

## Question 1
type: multipleChoice
question: What does "天气" mean?
options:
  - Season
  - Weather
  - Temperature
  - Climate
correctAnswer: Weather
explanation: "天气" (tiānqì) means "Weather" in Chinese.

type: fillInTheBlank
question: How do you say "Sunny" in Chinese?
